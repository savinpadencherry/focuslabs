The Cadence loop

Diagnose · Practice · Grade · Reinforce.

01 · Diagnose
A real task, not a quiz
Day one starts with a 30-minute scenario in the learner's actual domain. Every prompt, draft, and decision is captured at full fidelity.
02 · Practice
7 items at a time, AI-curated
The system identifies the top 2–3 gaps and serves a personalised practice path — short, specific, scaffolded.
03 · Grade
Multi-dimensional rubric
Source faithfulness, completeness, voice, hallucination detection, prompt efficiency — each scored with evidence and an actionable next step.
04 · Reinforce
90-second check-ins for 24 weeks
Spaced-repetition scheduler beats the midcycle dip. Items the learner struggled with return sooner; mastered items return later.
